<font color="#FFFF33">Elastic Stretch Loops</font> are elastic bands that are 
used to wrap and <font color="#FFFFFF">decorate 
<a href="/?cat|PBOX" class="FeatureLink">Plastic Boxes</a></font> or 
<font color="#FFFFFF">bundle envelopes and cards</font>. There are 2 
varieties of stretch loops, which include a 
<font color="#FFFFFF">round elastic</font> type in a variety of colors and a 
<font color="#FFFFFF">semi-clear flat stretchy vinyl</font> type.
