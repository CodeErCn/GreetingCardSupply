<font color="#FFFF33">Stick-on Hang Tabs</font> are used to 
<font color="#FFFFFF">hang products that don't have hangers</font> 
and are small and light enough to be hung up. Hang Tabs are 
<font color="#FFFFFF">clear</font> and have a 
<font color="#FFFFFF">moderate-strength adhesive</font> on the lower 
half of them.
